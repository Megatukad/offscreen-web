10 FAQ

Before you ask.

Will this sound like me, or like every other AI account?

Like you. Before any content gets made, I build a voice guide from how you actually write, and the tools pull from it. Generic output is an input problem. The voice guide is the input.

Can I just do this myself with ChatGPT?

You can. It takes about 30 to 40 hours: researching how to build a persona, testing prompt structures, working out what makes AI content stop sounding generic, building a content system from scratch, and documenting all of it so it holds together when you are not watching. The work is the obstacle, not the tools. This is that work, already done and structured to run reliably.

How is this different from hiring a freelancer on Fiverr?

A freelancer hands you an output. This hands you a system. A logo is not a brand. A few posts are not a content system. After this, you can brief a VA, a GPT, or anyone else to continue the brand without starting over. That is what documentation and structure give you that a one-off hire does not.

Do I have to be on camera or show my face?

No. That is the whole point. Your brand runs on a generated persona, or a stylized version of you if you prefer. You never film anything.

What does it cost to keep running after handoff?

About $20 a month for ChatGPT, where your prompt library lives. Notion is free for what you need. No retainer, and nothing paid to me after handoff.

What do I own at the end? Can I use it without you?

Everything lives in your accounts: your Notion workspace, your files, your prompts. I have no ongoing access after delivery unless you invite me back. A one-time fee means no subscriptions and no dependency. If I shut down tomorrow, your brand system keeps running.

What if I am not happy with it?

The voice guide and positioning are reviewed and approved before I build anything on top of them, so we agree on direction first. If a deliverable misses the brief we agreed at kickoff, I revise it until it matches. The Full Build includes a 14-day window for this.

What if I am pre-revenue, is this the right time?

It depends. If you know what you sell and who you sell it to, a brand system speeds up how fast people come to trust you. If you are still working out your offer, the build will not hold, because the content depends on knowing what you are moving people toward. If you are that early, start with the Founding Pilot.

How long does it take?

7 to 14 days for the Founding Pilot, 14 to 21 days for the Full Build, from kickoff to handoff. The timeline depends on you returning the intake and your feedback on time.

Which one should I pick?

The Founding Pilot if you want a fast start and to test the approach. The Full Build if you want the complete system, with a calendar and a content OS a VA can run without you.
